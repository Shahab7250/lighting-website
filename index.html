Our Story

Gaya's Premier
Event Decoration
Company

Founded in 1992 by the visionary decorator Deepak Ji, Deepak Lights & Events has grown from a small lighting shop in Gaya to Bihar's most trusted full-service event company. For over three decades, we have been the first choice for families seeking beauty, reliability and perfection for their most important life moments.

From grand wedding mandaps adorned with 10,000 fresh flowers to intimate birthday setups, from thundering DJ nights to elegant corporate galas — we bring equal passion and precision to every event, no matter its size.
